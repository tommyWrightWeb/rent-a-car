Do i need to book advance?
Yes, it is generally recommended that you book your car rental in advance, especially if you are traveling during peak season or if you need a specific type of vehicle. Even though last-minute rental cars can be cheaper, it might not be a good idea to procrastinate on booking, whether intentionally or not, and booking in advance can help ensure that you get the vehicle you need. KAYAK suggests that the best time to book a car is six to twelve months in advance if you’re traveling with a big group during peak season, but it’s okay to book closer to the rental date if you’re traveling in the off-season.
Why are your cars such good value?
Renting a car occasionally throughout the year could save you money compared to buying a luxury automobile to use every day. For example, a two-day luxury car rental in New York City in June 2020 would cost around $202 at Thrifty Car Rental and $227 at Enterprise. Buying an average entry-level luxury car costs $40,000 as of January 2020. Additionally, rental car companies follow maintenance schedules strictly to keep their vehicles in top condition, which is in their best interest to do so.
Is there discount for multi day deals?
Yes, our car rental companies offer discounts for multi-day rentals. Generally, the longer the rental period, the larger the discount. For example, we offer a discount of up to 20% off the base rate for rentals of 7 days or more. Additionally, our car rental companie offer special rates for weekend rentals or for rentals that span a holiday or other special event. It's always a good idea to check directly or to search for promo codes online to ensure you're getting the best deal possible.
